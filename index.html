Meet the Artist!

Hi, I'm Katrina Felicidario, a local artist from Naga and the creator behind Disen.You. Art has always been my way of telling stories, expressing emotions, and turning simple ideas into something meaningful. Every artwork in this gallery reflects a piece of that journey. I'm so glad you're here. Take a look around, explore each collection, and I hope you'll find something that inspires you. Welcome to my virtual art gallery! :)

Every piece has a story. Thank you for taking the time to discover mine.
